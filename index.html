Do you have curly or wavy hair?

A ton of people who have curly or wavy hair do not even realize it, they just think they have "frizzy" hair!

Hair Basics

Porosity of hair:

the more dense your hair is the higher porosity you have. This is just labeling how much space you have between your hair cuticles. The less space (denser hair) the higher porosity. The more space the higher porosity. Another rule of thumb is how fast does your hair get wet? It it immediately gets wet you have low porosity and if it takes you hair to get wet you have high porosity.

Curl Type:

Type 2 hair is waves.

Type 3 hair is more ringlettes.

Type 4 is z style curls.
